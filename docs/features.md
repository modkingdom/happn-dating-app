The Happn Dating app uniquely connects users with potential matches they've crossed paths with in real life, fostering genuine connections based on shared experiences. By utilizing location-based technology, Happn allows users to discover and interact with those they may have otherwise missed, creating an engaging and serendipitous dating experience.

## Core Features

### Location-Based Matching
- Connects users with others they have crossed paths with during the day.
- Displays a timeline of profiles encountered in real life.
- Offers the ability to see how many times you've crossed paths with someone.

### Interaction Options
- Send "likes" to show interest in a profile.
- Use the "Hello" feature to initiate conversations with potential matches.
- View mutual friends through Facebook for added context.

### Privacy Controls
- Users can control who sees their profiles and interactions.
- Option to hide distance from other users for more privacy.
- Profile visibility can be adjusted to preferred settings.
