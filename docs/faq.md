## General

**Is Happn Dating free?**  
Yes, the Happn Dating app is free to download and use. However, it offers in-app purchases and premium features that require a subscription.

**What is Happn Dating used for?**  
The Happn Dating app is designed to connect users with potential matches they have crossed paths with in real life. It uses location-based technology to show profiles of people who were nearby.

**Does Happn Dating work offline?**  
No, the Happn Dating app requires an internet connection to function, as it relies on real-time location data and user interactions.

## Download & Installation

**How do I download Happn Dating?**  
To download the Happn Dating app, visit the App Store or Google Play Store on your mobile device and search for "Happn Dating app." Click on the download button to install it.

**Is Happn Dating available on Windows / macOS / Linux?**  
No, the Happn Dating app is currently only available for mobile devices running iOS and Android. There is no desktop version for Windows, macOS, or Linux.

**Can I use Happn Dating without installing anything?**  
No, you must install the Happn Dating app on your mobile device to access its features and functionalities.

## Account & Pricing

**What's included in the free plan?**  
The free plan of Happn Dating includes:  
- Creating a profile  
- Browsing matches  
- Sending likes  
- Seeing who liked you (limited)

**How do I upgrade to a paid plan?**  
To upgrade to a paid plan in the Happn Dating app, go to your profile settings, select the subscription option, and choose your desired plan. Follow the prompts to complete the payment.

**Can I use Happn Dating for commercial projects on the free plan?**  
No, the Happn Dating app is intended for personal use only, and using it for commercial projects is not allowed under its terms of service.

## Troubleshooting

**Happn Dating is not loading — what should I do?**  
1. Check your internet connection to ensure you are connected.  
2. Restart the Happn Dating app.  
3. Clear the app cache in your settings.  
4. Ensure the app is updated to the latest version.  
5. Reinstall the app if the issue persists.

**How do I contact support?**  
To contact support for the Happn Dating app, go to the app's settings, select "Help," and follow the prompts to submit a support ticket or find contact information for customer service.
