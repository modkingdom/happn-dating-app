The Happn Dating app connects users with potential matches they've crossed paths with in real life. This guide provides a step-by-step process for downloading and setting up the app on both mobile devices and desktop.

## Option 1 — Mobile App (iOS and Android)

### Step 1 — Download the App
1. Open the App Store (iOS) or Google Play Store (Android) on your device.
2. Search for "Happn Dating app."
3. Tap on the "Install" or "Get" button to download the app.

### Step 2 — Create an Account
1. Open the Happn Dating app after installation.
2. Choose to sign up using your Facebook account, Google account, or phone number.
3. Follow the prompts to complete your profile setup, including uploading a profile picture and filling in your interests.

### Step 3 — Allow Location Access
1. Grant the app permission to access your location when prompted.
2. This allows Happn to connect you with users you've crossed paths with in real life.

---

## Option 2 — Web App

### Step 1 — Access the Website
1. Open a web browser on your computer.
2. Navigate to the official Happn website at www.happn.com.

### Step 2 — Sign Up for an Account
1. Click on the "Join Happn" button.
2. Enter your email address and create a password or sign up using your Facebook or Google account.

!!! tip "Free Version"
    The Happn Dating app offers a free version that includes basic features, but additional premium features are available through in-app purchases.

!!! warning "Requirements"
    Ensure your device has a stable internet connection and meets the minimum system requirements for the app to function properly. For mobile devices, iOS 11.0 or later, or Android 5.0 or later is recommended.
